👨‍👩‍👧 For Parents & Teachers
Kids World Academy is a free, browser-based learning platform designed to make education feel like play. No app to download, no account needed — just open and learn.
📚 What Your Child Will Learn
🔤
English & Literacy
Phonics, letter sounds, reading comprehension with mini-stories, sentence building, creative writing, vocabulary, idioms, and real-world conversations — structured from beginner to advanced.
➕
Maths & Numbers
Counting, addition, subtraction, multiplication tables, fractions, algebra, and timed Math Blitz challenges — with step-by-step voice explanations for every answer.
🌍
World Knowledge
Explore all 195 countries with their flags, capitals and regions. Learn geography, world history, and fascinating science facts through engaging quizzes.
🤖
AI Tutor — Nova
Nova is a friendly AI tutor powered by Claude. Your child can ask any question by typing or speaking — Nova gives patient, age-appropriate answers, motivates them, and explains concepts clearly.
🎮
Games & Rewards
Earn XP points, unlock reward titles, collect surprise gift boxes, and play Math Blitz, Count & Catch, Word Scramble, and Picture Word Quizzes — learning disguised as fun.
🎙️
Voice & Audio
Every lesson uses voice narration for pronunciation support. Children can also speak questions to Nova using the mic button — perfect for early readers and language learners.
